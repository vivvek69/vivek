Mental Health Assessment

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed by your responsibilities?

2. How often do you experience feelings of sadness or depression?

3. How often do you feel anxious or stressed?

4. How often do you feel disconnected from others?

5. How often do you find it hard to relax?

6. How often do you struggle with sleep (e.g., insomnia, trouble staying asleep)?

7. How often do you feel physically exhausted?

8. How often do you feel unmotivated to engage in activities you once enjoyed?
